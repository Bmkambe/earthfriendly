EARTH FRIENDLY

Global warming is an increase in the earth's temperature. It is as a result of human activities like indutrial activity. With its revolution, more toxic gases are released into the atmosphere, collects and absorbs sunlight and solar radiation, that in a period of time traps the heat and hikes temperature bringing about the greenhouse effect. It's not only industrial activity but also electricity production, agriculture, deforestation and our lifestyle, that contribtes to global warming. The earth's climate has changed which is marked by extreme droughts, wildfires, floods, tropical storms, and it is felt by all people. Thus, we are encouraged to change the way we go about our activities to protect and conserve the environment as we shall benefit from it too.


HEALTHY PRACTICES

Beach cleanups

Our coastal shores are highly polluted with all kinds of waste material. Some are deposited from rivers draining into the large water body, while others are deposited by people on the shore during their leisure time. Every year hundreds of people volunteer and take part in picking up trash from our sandy beaches. There are organizations that organiza such activities and welcome not only the locals but also interested environmental activists. Over 1500kg of trash is collcted every year. The trash contain debris and plastics that lie on the beach of which some are washed off shore into the water, and being toxic to the sea creatures.

Reuse

Reusing plastic waste that is non-biodegradable in creative ways is being encouraged. These materials are mostly used in the agricultural sector, where holes are bore into the bottles and a pipe is passed through it to allow water to sprinkle in all directions in the farm. Some may use plastic bottles as holders for their crops. Farmers may also apply the aerial method of farming, which is advantageous as the plants acquire enough elements it requires for it to flourish. These plastics are used to contain and conserve water in farms. Poeple have found creative ways of using these plastics even in their homes, as they play the role of flower vases.

Electric vehicles

They include cars and buses among others. Many countries are adopting the use of such;Nopia; for public transportation as they solve the climatic crisis like reduction of emission of toxic gases into the atmosphere that may destroy the ozone layer;which absorbs the sun's ultraviolent radiation. The harsh sun rays may cause skin disease. Toxic gases can be agents of respiratory diseases;cancer, asthma bronchitis etc. Public transportation reduces conjestion on roads that cause not only air but also sound pollution. Electirc vehicles are efficient as they can be charged anywhere, reducing the demand for diesel and petroleum extraction from the ore.

Recycling of plastics

Plastics can be recycled by melting and shaped into different forms of other useful products like cups, spoons, plates, pipes among others. Such items are durable, strong, lightweight, inexpensive to manufacture as it doesn't requuire much capital and become purposeful in our day-to-day lives. In the healthcare sectors, plastics can be formed into syringes, gloves, plastic bandages and containers where other tools are stored and prevent contamination and infection control. In Kenya, there is a ban of use of plastic polythene bags as they are non-biodegradable, and people have been encouraged to use the 'uhuru bags', as they are more eco-friendly.

Recycling of glass

Glass is also a waste material when disposed off wrongly. Shattered and scattered glasses is harmful to the earth and its inhabitants. Some companies have come up with creative ways of using these waste. The Kitengela hot glass company collects shattered glass, smoulders and turns them into fine pieces of art work and other useful products. It has also been a tourist attraction site as the decor of the place is quite attractive. Products like cups, windows, vases, bowls, pool floors, animal crafts, furniture among others are the final products of the waste glass materials. It has helped reduce the amount of glass that is toxic to the environment.

Use of solar panels

Sun is a natural source of energy and is renewable. People in the rural areas are quite fond of using solar panel for generating electricity. It is safe and clean and can be reused over many times. Solar radiation may be converted directly into electricity by solar cells and used in homesteads to power electric appliances.Solar energy has an important role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions and mitigating climate change, which is critical to protecting humans, wildlife, and ecosystems. Solar energy can also improve air quality and reduce water use from energy production.Solar energy encourages less toxic emissions from fossil fuels into our air.
