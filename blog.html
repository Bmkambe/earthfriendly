EARTH FRIENDLY

HEALTHY PRACTICES

Beach cleanups

Our coastal shores are highly polluted with inorganic waste that make it harmful for living sea creatures.Thus we have volunteers who help in monthly beach cleanups.

Reuse

Reusing plastic waste that is non-biodegradable in creative ways is being encouraged. This can be for domestic use, or agricultural purposes, just to mention a few.

Electric car

Adoptation of the use of electric vehicles which aid to reduce noise and air pollution, creating a cleanier and friendlier environment to live in.

Recycling

Disposing waste matter that could be non-biodegradable into recycle bins. This will reduce the nutrients to the soil that supports the plant life we depend on.

Use solar panels

Sun as a renewable source of energy helps to reduce the emission of toxic gases into the atmosphere and the destruction of the ozone layer and decreasing rate of global warming.

Demonstrations

Environmentalists have taken their love and care for the environment to the streets by demonstrating the need to conserve the environment.
